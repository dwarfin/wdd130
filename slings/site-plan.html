Purpose

Do you want a more comfortable way to carry a rifle? Do you want better stabilization when shooting? Do you want the ability to utilize both hands, yet keep your rifle out of the way but still within armâ€™s reach? My idea is to make fully customizable rifle slings that can fit any body frame. Rifle slings made from nylon strap and paracord. With this website I will be able to help show others what I am making and potentially even sell them.

Audience

The intended audience of this website is people who are looking for a custimizable rifle sling or want to know more about slings. They are looking for somthing that they will find functional, useful, as well as comfortable. The audience is most likely going to be people who are interested in outdoor shooting activities. People can access this website through a desktop, laptop, or mobile device.
