Rifle Slings

Why

Slings offer a variety of benefits to the user. Not only do they hold the rifle for you but can give you better mobility and accuracy. If you are outside carrying a rifle for an extended period of time it can get tiring fast. With a sling the weight of the rifle is transferred from your arms to your shoulders. Not only does this transfer the weight but now both of your hands are available. When shooting the rifle, you can utilize different positions as well as the sling to provide a brace which can help increase accuracy. Rifle slings provide a way to quickly put your rifle out of the way and when needed quickly grab it again.

How

These rifle slings are made from paracord and nylon webbing. First you choose weave. Different weaves have different amounts of paracord. Some weaves use one, two, or three different colors or strands of paracord. Different weaves offer overall different looks to the rifle sling. With the nylon webbing you can also choose any color you want. The nylon webbing also provides a way to make the sling adjustable to meet any size that is desired.

This sling featues a Covenant Oath weave which takes three different strands of paracord. The three colors chosen for this specific sling are burgundy and blue camo running down the outside with black running down the center and a vibrant pink nylon strap.
